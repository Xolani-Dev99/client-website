Registration at Romphe

Ready to Join the Romphe Family?

Registering your child is simple and stress-free! Here’s how you can secure their place at our creche:

  1. Visit Us In Person:
    Come meet our staff, take a tour, and see the happy faces of our little learners in action. We’ll walk you through everything you need.
  2. Collect or Request a Registration Form:
    Forms are available at the centre or can be emailed to you upon request.
  3. Prepare the Following Documents:
    • 📄 Copy of child’s birth certificate
    • 📄 Copy of parent/guardian’s ID
    • 💉 Immunisation record
    • 📝 Completed registration form
    • 📬 Proof of residence
    • 💳 Registration fee payment slip

We’re excited to welcome you and your little one into the Romphe family!
